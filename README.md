# 🌍 Global Time Companion

Your friendly world clock that makes coordinating across time zones feel like a breeze.

## ✨ What This Does

Ever struggled to find the perfect time for a meeting with colleagues in New York, London, and Tokyo? This tool is your answer. It's a simple, beautiful time zone converter that helps you:

- **See everyone's time at a glance** - Just set your local time and watch as it updates for cities around the world
- **Find perfect meeting times** - Automatically detects when everyone is available (9 AM - 6 PM in their timezone)
- **Add cities instantly** - Type any major city name and it's there
- **Stay organized** - Drag and drop cities to arrange them how you like
- **Save your preferences** - Your favorite cities are remembered for next time

## 🎯 Who This Is For

- **Remote teams** working across different time zones
- **Digital nomads** keeping track of clients worldwide
- **Friends and family** staying in touch across continents
- **Anyone** who's ever asked "What time is it there right now?"

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Open the HTML file** in your browser - no installation needed!
2. **Set your local time** (defaults to India time)
3. **Add cities** you care about using the search box
4. **Drag to reorder** cities however you prefer
5. **Click "Sync Now"** to update to current time instantly

## 🎨 Features That Make Life Easier

### Smart Meeting Planner
The "Perfect Meeting Time" feature automatically scans all 24 hours to find when everyone is working (9 AM - 6 PM their time). No more back-and-forth emails trying to coordinate!

### Beautiful, Adaptive Design
- **Dynamic backgrounds** that change based on time of day
- **Glass morphism effects** for a modern, clean look
- **Responsive design** that works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops

### Thoughtful Details
- **Working hours indicator** - See at a glance if someone is available or after hours
- **24/12 hour toggle** - Use whichever format you prefer
- **Local storage** - Your favorite cities are saved automatically
- **Smooth animations** - Everything feels fluid and delightful

## 🛠️ Tech Stuff (For the Curious)

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript** - No frameworks, no dependencies
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **International Time Zone API** for accurate time calculations
- **LocalStorage API** for saving your preferences
- **Drag & Drop API** for reordering cities

## 🌟 Pro Tips

1. **Pin the tab** - Keep it open all day for quick time checks
2. **Use the search** - Try typing just the first few letters of a city
3. **Clear and rebuild** - Use "Clear All" to start fresh with a new project team
4. **Meeting planning** - Adjust the base time to see how it affects everyone's schedule
